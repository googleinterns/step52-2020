What is Contact Tracing?

Contact tracing is the process of identification of persons who may have come into contact with an infected person and subsequent collection of further information about these contacts.

TL;DR If you're positive for COVID-19, we help let people you've been in contact with know.

How is Contact Tracing performed by Online Contact Tracing Co.?

We start by asking you for your data. You can either sign-in with your Google Account and grant us access

to some of your data, like your Contacts and Calendar, or you can provide this information manually!

Then we identify people and businesses that you may want to let know. After you've confirmed these,

we'll send them an email letting them know! The email can be anonymous or made in your name!

TL;DR We use your data to determine a list of people and businesses to contact. Then we send them an email letting them know!

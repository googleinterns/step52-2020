FAQ

Contact tracing is the process of identification of persons who may have come into contact with an infected person and subsequent collection of further information about these contacts.

TL;DR If you're positive for COVID-19, we help let people you've been in contact with know.


We start by asking you for your data. You can either sign-in with your Google Account and grant us access to some of your data, or you can provide this info manually! Then we identify possible contacted individuals and businesses. After you've confirmed these, we'll send them an (optionally anonymous) email letting them know!

TL;DR We use your data to determine a list of people and businesses to contact. Then we send them an email letting them know!


Contact Tracing helps prevent outbreaks and slow down the spread of contagious diseases by attempting to warn people as soon as possible. It helps catch and control new outbreaks before they are able to grow, effectively stopping the disease in its tracks.

TL;DR Contact Tracing catches outbreaks early in the process and stops the disease's spread.


Online Contact Tracing helps provide a thorough and efficient method of identifying contacts that may have been infected. We also provide an easy way of communicating with these contacts with customizable messages supported in different languages.

TL;DR Online Contact Tracing provides a thorough, efficient, and secure way of performing Contact Tracing.
